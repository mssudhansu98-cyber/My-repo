# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

##  General Questions

### Q1: Can I cancel my ticket?
Yes, you can cancel tickets from the **My Trips** section.

### Q2: How will I receive my ticket?
You will receive your ticket via email and SMS.

### Q3: What if payment fails?
- Check internet connection
- Retry payment
- Contact support if issue persists

### Q4: Can I modify my booking?
Yes, depending on airline policies.

##  Support
For further assistance, contact customer support via the app.
