# Search Flights

## Steps to Search Flights

1. Open the MakeMy Trip Appilcation
2. Tap on **Flights**
3. Enter the following details:
   - Departure city
   - Destination city
   - Travel date
4. Tap **Search**

## Result
A List of available flights will be displayed.

## Notes
- Ensure correct tavel dates
- Use filters to refine results
